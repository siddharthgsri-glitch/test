04 · Mission brief

AI for Bharat.
Small tools, real problems.

I want to use AI to help with the ordinary stuff — the messy, everyday problems that Indians run into but nobody makes an app for. Not big flashy things. Small, useful, kind tools that actually work in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, and the way people really talk.
