01 · The problem

What KrishiScan is trying to fix.

Small farmers lose entire harvests to diseases that could be caught in a day. Agri-officers can't cover every village, and generic Google searches don't work for the specific crop varieties grown here.

KrishiScan lets any farmer with a phone photograph a leaf, a stem, or a fruit and get an on-device diagnosis in seconds. The recommendation is local — accounts for the soil, the season, and the pesticides actually available at the nearest agri-shop.

Design principle
"A phone camera can save a season."
02 · How it works

Three steps, no learning curve.

01

Point & scan

Open KrishiScan, aim at the affected leaf or crop, and hold steady. The model works offline once downloaded.

02

Read the diagnosis

See the likely disease with confidence %, alternative possibilities, and how urgent it is. All in the farmer's language.

03

Get a local plan

Get a step-by-step treatment plan using inputs available nearby — plus a link to the closest Krishi Vigyan Kendra if it's serious.
