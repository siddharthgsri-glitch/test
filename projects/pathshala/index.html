01 · The problem

What PathshalaAI is trying to fix.

Kids in Indian government schools often learn in one language but read reference material in another. Their science textbook is in Kannada but the good YouTube explainer is in English. Their Hindi grammar guide has zero examples that feel like home.

PathshalaAI takes any textbook page — English, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi — and rewrites it in the kid's stronger language, with local examples. Not just translated — reworded so it actually makes sense to a 10-year-old.

Design principle
"Understanding shouldn't depend on which language your textbook is in."
02 · How it works

Three steps, no learning curve.

01

Scan the page

Take a photo of a textbook page or paste text. Any Indian language, any subject.

02

Pick the target

Choose the language and reading level. PathshalaAI adapts vocabulary, examples, and even units to match.

03

Read & ask questions

Get a rewritten version + a small tutor chat for anything the kid still doesn't understand.
