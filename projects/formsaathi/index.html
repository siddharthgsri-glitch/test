01 · The problem

What FormSaathi is trying to fix.

Every Indian household has a story about a form gone wrong. Pension, Aadhaar update, ration card, school admission — dense PDFs full of English legalese that grandparents, first-time applicants, and small business owners have to guess through.

FormSaathi reads the form for you. Take a photo, tell it what you're trying to do, and it walks you through every field in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Bengali — whichever language you actually think in. No jargon. No skipped boxes.

Design principle
"Nobody should lose a benefit because a form was too hard to read."
02 · How it works

Three steps, no learning curve.

01

Snap the form

Photograph any government form or upload a PDF. The AI reads the fields, checkboxes, and instructions.

02

Chat in your language

Answer simple questions in Hindi, Kannada, Tamil or English. The assistant maps each answer to the right field.

03

Get a filled form

Download or print the completed form, ready to submit. FormSaathi also flags missing documents you'll need.
